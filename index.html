How It Works

1

Registration

  • Must be 18 years or older and legally eligible to work
  • Basic email and internet skills required
  • Traits: honesty, observance, reliability, and availability for 3–4 hours per week
2

Assignment Selection

Once you register, assignments are given based on availability and location. You'll receive clear instructions on where to shop or inquire.

Potential Assignments Include:

  • Financial services
  • Logistics companies
  • Retail stores
  • And more…

Each assignment comes with specific instructions, timelines, and expected outcomes.

3

Mystery Shopping

Imagine entering a store, acting like a regular shopper, but secretly observing, evaluating, and reporting.

What You’ll Do:

  • Visit assigned stores as a customer
  • Discreetly assess the experience
  • Blend in and take notes
4

Detailed Reporting

After each assignment, you'll complete a short form about your experience. You'll be asked about:

  • Overall experience
  • Staff professionalism
  • Wait times

You’re not just shopping—you’re playing a key role in helping companies improve!

5

Compensation

We send you a certified check that covers both your shopping budget and payment.

Simply cash the check, complete the assignment, and submit your report.

Typical earnings range from $500 to $700 per week.
